M7 - Web Renderer

GraphViz is great, but being able to see things like the description of your deliverables and their current state is something that most users are probably going to find quite useful. Pair that with a need to make tinkering and experimentation easy and there are few things better than an interactive website to show your road map. The goal here is to produce something which is easy and pleasurable to use, which shows your road map in a format which can be understood and makes interacting with it as low-fuss as possible.
